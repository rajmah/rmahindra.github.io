I am a Senior Associate Researcher at NEC Labs America, Princeton, NJ. I design and implement systems that strive to achieve end-to-end LTE network virtualization and in-network video streaming enhancements. I have aquired significant knowledge of the LTE network stack, including hands-on experience in building prototypes. Prior to joining NEC, I earned my Masters degree from Winlab, Rutgers University in 2007. I was part of the Winlab team, working on GENI projects, including the design of an open virtualized WiMAX basestation, WiFi virutalization and the integration of the Orbit and PlanetLab testbeds.
